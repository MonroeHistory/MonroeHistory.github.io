Clear Creek, Indiana, is a small unincorporated village in which is located four miles south of Bloomington Indiana, on a Monroe County road one-half mile west of Indiana Highway number 37. People who live in Clear Creek are employed in Bloomington, own their own businesses in Clear Creek, or are retired farmers. Surrounding the town is some of the richest farm land in Southern Indiana. The main buildings which compose the business section of Clear Creek are two grocery stores, a school, a railroad station, a post office and a church.
